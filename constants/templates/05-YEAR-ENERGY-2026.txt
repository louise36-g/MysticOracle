YEAR ENERGY TEMPLATE - 2026
=============================
Used in: Reading 3 (Year Energy Reading)

2026 = 2+0+2+6 = 10 = Wheel of Fortune
Further reduced: 1+0 = 1 = The Magician

So 2026 carries DUAL energy:
- Wheel of Fortune (10): Cycles, change, fate turning, karma
- The Magician (1): Manifestation, will, new beginnings, creation

Every person's Year Reading weaves this dual energy with their birth cards.
